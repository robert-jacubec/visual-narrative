United States Population and United States House Prices



Introduction:

This set of graphs focus on select parameters that influence house prices.
Here are some of the parameters that influence house prices:

  • Population
  • Mortgage Interest Rates
  • House size

About This Graph:

The main parameter that is positively correlated to house prices in the United States is population.
As one can see, the United States population rises alongside the average house price.
The positive correlation between United States house prices and United States population is due to
increased demand for homes; as population rises so will demand rise for homes. When the demand for homes increase
the house prices will increase as well.
