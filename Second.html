30 Year Mortgage Interest Rates and House Prices


About This Graph:

Another parameter related to increasing house prices is the 30 year mortgage rate.
The way mortgage rates affect house prices is by allowing money to be more accessible to consumers.
Accessability of money is indirectly proportional to house prices. The indirect proportionality is due
to the fact that if interest rates are lower, then more consumers are willing to borrow money to buy homes.
If more of the population is buying homes then the demand of homes will be driven up and so will prices of homes.
