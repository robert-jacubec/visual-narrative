Housing Size and House Prices


About This Graph:

Another parameter that is positively correlated with house prices is the house size itself. Homes have become
larger and larger over time. Thus, the house price will be affect by increased house size.
Americans have been demanding larger and larger homes. So, house builders have been building larger
and larger homes to meet the demand of consumers. Thus, house prices also increased to reflect
the increasing sizes of homes.
